Alternate Universes

These are the current Alternate Universes (also known as AUs) I’ve either created or have my OCs be in them:

  • Kirby AUs:

    • Discord AUs (these ones are not made by me):
      • Starboard
      • Stargate
  • Other AUs:
    • Lotus Princess AU

Fallen AU

In this AU, Falx loses her foster daughter, Jamba Dee, prematurely. Consumed by her grief, she pushes those that care about her away and eventually finds one of the Jamba Heart shards that still exist within the world. Falx gets possessed by the dark heart piece, and goes insane, leading her to start collecting wings for some unknown reason. This includes Squall's wings. Many characters die in this AU.

Squall is not having a good time after losing her wings to Falx.

LMKirby AU

A crossover somewhat roleswap AU where characters of Lego Monkie Kid and Kirby combine together based on their similarities. For instance, Kirby and MK/Qi Xiaotian are combined into a single character, MKirby.

Starboard

A college AU of the Discord Starboard server. Currently appears to be on hiatus.

Status of my characters in Starboard:

  • Squall: an energetic and enthusiastic student that’s a bit dense.
  • Falx: a quiet student overriden with anxiety.
  • Loga: a language teacher (especially in dead languages) who is often forgetful.

Stargate

An AU set in the distant future of the Kirby Universe, where most of the Kirby cast no longer exists. A new organization, called Stargate, has risen up after the fall of the GSA to keep order.

this is probably the least developed au oop

Status of my OCs in Stargate:

  • Squall: A member/soldier of Stargate. She is trained by someone else instead of Meta Knight
  • Loga: Not a member of Stargate. They live on a desolate planet devoid of all lifeforms except for themself. They have probably have or will attract the attention of Stargate at some point.

Lotus Princess AU

A semi-LMK AU where Nameless (Called 睡莲/Shuìlián in this AU) is the sworn sibling of Nezha. Unfortunately, a human with the power to bend reality at will is not exactly welcomed within the land of gods...
